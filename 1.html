Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Drive Me

How do I pay a traffic fine using the app?
To pay a traffic fine, simply log into the Driver App, navigate to "My Fines" section, select the fine you wish to pay, and choose your preferred payment method. The app supports credit/debit cards, online banking, and mobile wallets. After successful payment, a digital receipt will be generated and stored in the app for future reference.
Is my payment information secure?
Yes, Drive Me uses bank-grade encryption to protect all payment information. We do not store any credit card details on our servers. All transactions are processed through PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways with additional security measures like tokenization and multi-factor authentication.
How can I retrieve my license after paying a fine?
After paying your fine through the app, you can visit any police station with your digital receipt and identification to retrieve your license. In some areas, we've implemented a digital license system where you'll receive a digital permit valid until you can collect your physical license.
What if I don't have a smartphone?
While the app offers the most convenient experience, you can also access your fines and make payments through our web portal using any internet-connected device. Additionally, the traditional payment methods at post offices remain available for those who prefer them.
Is the app available in multiple languages?
Yes, Drive Me is available in Sinhala, Tamil, and English to serve all citizens of Sri Lanka. You can change your language preference in the app settings at any time.
